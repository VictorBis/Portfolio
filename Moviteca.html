What is Moviteca?

A project that wants to encourage reading and acquiring culture in Mexico

Moviteca is one of the projects of the Laboratorio Experimental de Artes y Aplicaciones A.C., which is an association that wants to encourage reading and acquiring culture in the public, private and educational sectors through different programs.

Moviteca is only in social networks, and they actually have a base of followers, but they want to expand their product to impact more people.

The Problem

Moviteca's readers need to consume all the content in one place because they sometimes miss their posts on social medial

In average, mexicans read 3.4 books per year. People don't tend to read and every year the number of readers decreases. Moviteca attacks the problem by creating content about reading and culture, involving recommendations, tips, articles, interviews, reports, images, videos and more, which they share on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.

Their followers ask them to give them an option that allows them to read all this content in a different platform, because they sometimes miss it and it's not the most comfortable way to consume its content.

Goals

The association defined some goals to be accomplished

  • Create a platform that is referent in the encouraging of reading and culture.
  • Create and show all the content they've worked on in a way their audience can explore more easily.
  • Attract more people to the project, creating a bigger community.
  • Create a platform that is clean, contemporary and shows order and professionalism.

User Personas

Based on the research done by the association, I defined the personas

There are three target users, people interested in literature in general, people interested in promoting reading in children and people who work in companies where we provide our services.

Competitor Analysis

A lot of products with the same goals hasn't been updated in years

It was hard to find products with similar goals and information, most of them were only to buy books. In the end, I did the analysis with apps from government, schools and editorials, which are direct competitors. Almost all of them have apps that has an interface that looks old or with features that doesn't work, but I found some important information that will help me to build a better product.

The Solution

A website and an app that lets people explore all the content that Moviteca offers

The website and the app will allow the users to explore the content in a more comfortable way, giving them a clearer organization, easier navigation and better ways to consume the content than social networks provides.

Moviteca is funded by the services that it provides to different companies and the app will display exclusive content for their workers. This is a feature that will allow them to get more clients, subsist and maintain most of its content for free.

Key Features

There are some features that the app must have

  • Clear organization. There are a lot of information that needs to be well organized in order to facilitate the navigation through the app.
  • Search Bar. It helps to easily find any kind of information the users wants to get.
  • Login for enterprises. This is a feature that lets users get special content.

Design System

A multiplatform app

One of the things the association emphasized was they wanted a fast app, which the loading time is the minimum possible. This depends on the implementation, but it can be improved with things like the typography, that's why I choose the default operative system as the typography.

The red color is the main color of the association's logo, that's why I choose it, and complemented with black and white to continue the way people read on their smartphones.

The Final Design

The design of the app

Home

The home section contains all the free content that any user can explore. With its horizontal scrollable menu users can explore through the categories of the information and the filter option lets them explore by genres (biography, crime, fiction, etc).

Children

The children section has the same structure as the home section, they'll differentiate each other by the content each one displays.

Search

There's a lot of content available, so the search section will help the users to find the information they're looking for. I decided to add suggestions behind the search bar in order to give more options, we can't display smart suggestions depending on the user's behavior, so the suggestions will be defined by the necessities and goals of the association at the moment.

Gallery

The association needed to show all the media they have, this section is similar as other apps shows its media. At the moment, due to the way the content is organized, it's not possible to add a menu that can helps to explore content by category, but it might be an option that can be added in an update, depending on the feedback we'll get.

Enterprise

Enterprises are the ones that keep alive this project, so they receive exclusive content depending on the goals they have. The login form just asks for the code we give the employees of the enterprises that work with us. If someone forgets the code, there's no way to get it inside the app due to we don't have the tools to automatize it at the moment.

Enterprises get new employees and loses some constantly, with the structure we have there's no way to deny the access to an employee that doesn't work for an enterprise anymore, that's why I decided to change the code every month, so we can keep restrung the access.

Article

The main content of the app are articles. A lot of our publications in social networks are shared, that's why I decided to add this option inside the app. In order to give the article more space, I decided to remove the tab bar when the users scroll down, and it'll appears when they scroll up.

We received comments of some people who told us that a problem they had is that when they're reading a large article, they sometimes forget the title or the main subject, that's why I decided to always show what the user is reading.

What I learned

Designing an app that has users waiting for its launch

  • I'm going to develop this app, so I had to design it considering my skills and the time it will take me to implement each feature.
  • Working with people who aren't involved in this field is a little hard, sometimes the communication is a problem, but the way we can complement our ideas is amazing.
  • Working completely remotely is a new experience, but I liked the way we can work and it helped me to be more responsible.
  • Working directly with the client is hard, they sometimes ask for things that can't be achieved with the resources. Sometimes I asked for information and had to wait weeks to receive it, the experience wasn't the best at the beginning, but I had to learn to deal with it and collaborate with them in the best way possible.
