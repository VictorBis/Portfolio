What is Cinépolis?

The biggest cinema in Mexico and Latin America, and one of the companies that sells more tickets in the world

I love Cinépolis, almost everyone I know prefer it over other companies because of the quality and service they provide, but its app isn't what people want and need.

The Problem

Why a big company has an app with only two stars?

People don't use the Cinépolis app because it doesn't work as people expected, the reviews in the App Store are full of complaints. Its purpose is to speed up the process of getting tickets and food, but people still prefer to go to the ticket office.

App Analysis

I did an analysis of the app to understand how it works, its navigation and architecture

I explored every aspect of the app, the way you can interact with it, what you can and can't do, their features and the purpose of each one of them.

User Interviews

I interviewed 4 people who are frequent clients of Cinépolis

From the people I interviewed, two are in their 40s and the other two in their 20s. My goal was to know their experience with the app and how they use it.

Here are some key insights:

  • They go to Cinépolis at least once a month, but none of them has bought tickets with the app in the most recent ones.
  • All of them have downloaded the app, but just one used it to buy tickets.
  • They don't use it because it's almost impossible to use.
  • One of them uninstalled the app.
  • They don't recommend the app, two of them don't use it anymore, and the other one just use it to see the schedules of the movies.
  • If they buy tickets online, they buy them on the website.

User Reviews

I complemented the information I got with the reviews of the users in the App Store

A lot of people has used the app, they have over 10k reviews, but unfortunately the comments aren't the best ones.

Usability Test

I did a usability test to see how users interact with the app and to identify more pain points

I did the test with the people I interviewed, asking them to do the most common tasks a user can do in the app.

I defined the next tasks:

  • Buy a ticket for the movie "Amores Perros".
  • Change your location to see the schedule of movies in the cinemas located downtown.
  • Explore the catalog of presales.
  • Check your level and how many points you have.

Pain Ponts

The main problem is in the code, but it still has problems that can be solved before implementation

With the analysis I did at the beginning, the interviews, reviews and the usability test, I confirmed that the app has a lot of problems that can be solved only with code, and it's the main reason why the app isn't used.

I found some pain points:

  • Can't explore the cinemas out of your town.
    Users want to explore the schedule of cinemas that are outside their town, but they can't do it.
  • The process of buying a ticket is large.
    Users didn't like to introduce all their data in the process, because they've already introduced it when they logged in.
  • The button "Siguiente" isn't visible.
    In the process of buying a ticket, some users didn't notice the next button.
  • Selecting the seats is confusing.
    When you go to the cinema they always indicate you where is the screen, surprisingly this is really necessary, as some users that helped me with the test dind't notice it.
  • After you buy a ticket, there's no confirmation.
    I found two comments in the App Store that mentioned that when you finish your purchase, the app doesn't show you a confirmation.
  • Can't add or update a credit card.
    Users couldn't add or update a credit card as they can do it in other apps, unless they do it in the process of buying a ticket. This form was complicated for two people that participated in my test.
  • Have to explore all the catalog to find a movie.
    The only way of finding a movie is scrolling through all the content.
  • They ask you for a PIN to see your club information.
    In order to see how many points, visits and all your club information, you need a PIN that some people don't know.
